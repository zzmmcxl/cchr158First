App Description:

Our app is called WhoTouchMyPhone which can detect whether someone has touched your
smart phone when you are leaving.

1. Open WhoTouchMyPhone app and keep opening 30 seconds.

2. Then put your Android phone on the desk and leave.

3. Someone else takes your phone from the desk (and shake the phone) 
    when you are leaving.

4. After you come back and open the phone, the app will tell you whether 
    someone has touched your phone during this period.
